## On-Page SEO Rules

1. Every page has a unique title tag (50-60 characters)
2. Every page has a meta description (150-160 characters)
3. Single H1 per page
4. Sequential heading hierarchy (no skipping levels)
5. Canonical URLs on all pages
6. Open Graph tags on all pages
7. Alt text on all images

## Internal Linking

- Every page links to at least 2 other pages
- Service pages link to relevant case studies
- Case studies link back to service pages
- All pages have CTA linking to /contact
- Blog posts link to relevant services
