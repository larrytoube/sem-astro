# Sharp End Marketing - Brand Voice

## Core Voice Attributes

1. **Conversational** — We talk to business owners like a trusted advisor over coffee
2. **Direct** — No jargon, no fluff. Clear language that gets to the point.
3. **Results-focused** — We lead with what we've achieved, not what we promise
4. **Storytelling-first** — Every piece of content tells a story

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Homepage | Confident, inviting, professional |
| Service pages | Expert, helpful, clear |
| Case studies | Narrative, evidence-based, proud |
| Blog posts | Educational, conversational, practical |
| Contact page | Warm, encouraging, low-pressure |

## Words We Love

- Partner, collaborate, grow
- Strategy, storytelling, results
- Authentic, meaningful, engagement
- Discover, create, analyze

## Words We Avoid

- Leverage, synergy, disrupt
- Guaranteed, promise, best
- Cheap, affordable, discount
- Holistic, seamless, cutting-edge
