# Sharp End Marketing - Audience Personas

## Primary: Small Business Owner

**Demographics**: 30-55 years old, owns a business with 5-50 employees, based in Vancouver/BC area or remote
**Goals**: Generate leads, build brand awareness, get ROI on marketing spend
**Pain Points**: Limited marketing budget, no time to manage marketing, overwhelmed by options
**Decision Criteria**: Proven results, personal attention, fair pricing, understanding of their business
**Content Preferences**: Case studies, clear service descriptions, process explanations

## Primary: Entrepreneur/Startup Founder

**Demographics**: 25-45 years old, launching or scaling a new venture
**Goals**: Establish brand identity, build digital presence, reach first customers
**Pain Points**: No marketing expertise in-house, need to move fast, limited budget
**Decision Criteria**: Strategic thinking, flexibility, willingness to educate
**Content Preferences**: Blog posts, strategy guides, quick wins

## Secondary: Marketing Manager

**Demographics**: 28-45 years old, works at a mid-size company
**Goals**: Outsource specific functions (social, SEO, design), hit KPIs
**Pain Points**: Internal bandwidth, need specialized expertise, reporting expectations
**Decision Criteria**: Track record, communication quality, measurable deliverables
**Content Preferences**: Service details, case studies with metrics, process documentation
