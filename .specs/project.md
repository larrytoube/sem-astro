# Sharp End Marketing Site - Project Specification

## Vision

Build a marketing website for Sharp End Marketing that showcases services, case studies, and thought leadership. Replace the existing Webflow site at sharpendmarketing.com with a fast, modern Astro-based site deployed to Vercel.

## Target Audience

### Primary Segments

1. **Small-to-Medium Business Owners** — Looking for marketing support to grow their brand
   - Motivations: Lead generation, brand awareness, ROI on marketing spend
   - Content preference: Case studies, clear service descriptions, results-focused

2. **Entrepreneurs & Startups** — Need strategic marketing from day one
   - Motivations: Brand positioning, digital presence, audience growth
   - Content preference: Process explanations, pricing clarity, quick wins

### Secondary Segments

3. **Marketing Managers** — Looking to outsource specific marketing functions
4. **Referral Partners** — Other agencies and complementary service providers

## Scope

### In Scope

- Homepage with value proposition and service overview
- Individual service pages (7 services)
- Case studies for existing clients
- Blog for thought leadership
- About page with team information
- Contact page with form
- SEO optimization
- CI/CD pipeline with quality checks

### Out of Scope

- E-commerce or online payments
- Client portal or dashboard
- CRM integration (future)
- Multi-language support

## Milestones

1. **Foundation** — Astro scaffold, design system, layouts, components
2. **Content** — All pages with real content from Webflow site
3. **Quality** — Eval scripts, CI pipeline, accessibility checks
4. **Launch** — Vercel deployment, DNS cutover from Webflow
5. **Optimization** — Performance tuning, SEO refinement, blog content
