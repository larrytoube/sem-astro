# Sharp End Marketing - Brand Specification

## Brand Identity

**Sharp End Marketing** — Always written with both words capitalized. Abbreviated as "Sharp End" or "SEM" in informal contexts.

### Tagline

"Your partner in success"

### Brand Promise

We are a boutique digital marketing agency that connects clients with their target audience through strategic storytelling, creating meaningful engagement and measurable results.

### Leadership

**Chris Ricard** — Co-founder. Serial entrepreneur. Marketing strategy first.
**Larry Toube** — Co-founder. Design thinking, crafting ideas into impactful realities.

## Terminology

### Always Use

| Term | Context |
|------|---------|
| Sharp End Marketing | Full company name |
| storytelling | Central to our approach — marketing starts with strategy and finishes with storytelling |
| boutique agency | We work with a small number of clients |
| outsourced marketing department | How we position our services |
| brand strategy | Our foundational service |

### Never Use

| Avoid | Reason | Use Instead |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| "full-service agency" | We are boutique, not full-service | "boutique agency" |
| "guaranteed results" | No marketing results are guaranteed | "results-driven approach" |
| "cheap" or "affordable" | Devalues our positioning | "investment in marketing" |

## Writing Style

### Principles

1. **Conversational & Direct** — Write like you're talking to a business owner over coffee
2. **Results-focused** — Lead with outcomes, not features
3. **Storytelling-first** — Every piece of content tells a story
4. **Honest & Transparent** — No hype, no empty promises

### Formatting Conventions

- H1 and H2: Title Case
- H3 and below: Sentence case
- Oxford comma: Always
- Numbers: Spell out one through nine, numerals for 10+

## Visual Identity

### Fonts

- **Inter** — Body text. Clean, professional, highly readable.
- **Comfortaa** — Headings, accent, and logo font. Rounded, friendly, distinctive. Weight 500 for headings.

### Colors

- Teal: rgba(8, 109, 114, 0.82) — Primary brand accent (buttons)
- Teal Solid: #086d72 — Links, highlights
- Teal Dark: #013941 — Footer, CTA sections, dark backgrounds
- Teal Accent: #009688 — Decorative text highlights
- Heading: #0A0D14 — Heading text
- Text Body: #525866 — Body text
- Text Muted: rgba(10, 13, 20, 0.45) — Secondary text
- Background: #ffffff (main), #F6F8FA (sections)
- Border: rgba(9, 14, 29, 0.1)

### Design Principles

- Clean, professional, uncluttered
- Generous whitespace
- Card-based layouts for services and case studies
- Full-width CTA sections with dark backgrounds

## Accessibility

- All text meets WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 normal text, 3:1 large text)
- All images require meaningful alt text
- Interactive elements require visible focus indicators
- Heading hierarchy must be sequential
- Use semantic HTML landmarks
