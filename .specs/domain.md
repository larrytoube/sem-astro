# Sharp End Marketing - Domain Model

## Page Types

### Homepage

**Purpose**: First impression, showcase services, build trust, drive contact.

**Required Sections**:
1. Hero — "Your partner in success" with CTA
2. Services grid — 7 numbered service tiles
3. Why Sharp End — Storytelling positioning
4. Our Process — 3-phase methodology (Discover, Create, Analyze)
5. Success Stories — Featured case studies
6. Blog preview — Recent articles
7. CTA — "Ready to Elevate Your Brand?"

### Service Page

**Purpose**: Detailed overview of a specific service offering.

**Required Sections**:
1. Hero — Service name and tagline
2. What We Offer — 4 specific deliverables
3. Why This Matters — Value explanation
4. CTA — Contact us

### Case Study Page

**Purpose**: Demonstrate results through client success stories.

**Required Sections**:
1. Hero — Client name and project summary
2. Overview — Client background
3. The Challenge — Problem statement
4. Our Approach — Solution description
5. Results — Metrics and outcomes
6. CTA — "Have a Similar Challenge?"

### About Page

**Purpose**: Build trust through team and values.

**Required Sections**:
1. Hero — "Less really is more"
2. Agency description
3. Qualifying questions
4. Why Sharp End Marketing
5. Team cards
6. CTA

### Contact Page

**Purpose**: Convert visitors to leads.

**Required Sections**:
1. Heading and description
2. Contact form (React, client:visible)

### Blog Index

**Purpose**: Showcase thought leadership.

**Required Sections**:
1. Hero
2. Post grid
3. CTA
