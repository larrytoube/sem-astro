# Sharp End Marketing - Content Strategy

## Brand Voice

**Conversational & Professional** — Friendly but knowledgeable. We speak like a trusted advisor, not a salesperson.
**Storytelling-First** — Marketing starts with strategy and finishes with storytelling.
**Results-Driven** — Every claim is backed by outcomes. Show what we achieved for clients.
**Honest & Direct** — No jargon, no hype. Clear language that business owners understand.

### Voice Examples

| Instead of | Write |
|-----------|-------|
| "We leverage synergies to optimize your funnel" | "We help you get more customers through smart marketing" |
| "Our holistic approach drives engagement" | "We build a marketing plan that actually works — and show you the results" |
| "Utilize our cutting-edge solutions" | "We use the latest tools to save you time and get better results" |

## Content Pillars

1. **Brand Strategy & Storytelling** — Every successful marketing effort starts with a clear strategy and compelling story

## Content Rules

1. **Lead with outcomes** — "30% increase in enrollments" not "We executed a campaign"
2. **Be specific** — Use real numbers, client names, and timeframes
3. **Storytelling arc** — Challenge → Approach → Results on every case study
4. **CTA on every page** — Above the fold and at the bottom
5. **Internal linking** — Every page links to at least 2 other pages
